Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI measurement?
Our AI provides an estimated measurement within approximately ±10–20% of actual roof size — accurate enough for a solid ballpark before getting contractor quotes. For certified measurements, a licensed contractor will measure on-site.
What's included in the free report?
Your PDF report includes AI-estimated roof square footage, roof complexity and pitch assessment, material and labor cost estimate range, and an estimated contractor completion timeline.
Is it really free?
Yes — completely free for homeowners. No credit card, no account required. Just enter your address and get your report.
What roofing materials do you cover?
We provide cost estimates for Asphalt Shingle, Metal, Tile, Wood Shake, and Flat/TPO roofing — the five most common residential roofing materials.
Does this work for any address in the US?
Yes — RoofGrid AI works for any residential address in the United States using Mapbox satellite imagery.
